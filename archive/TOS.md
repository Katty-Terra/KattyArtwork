**Payment**
Pay all at the beginning. 
At values greater than 200$, there is a possibility for monthly payments. 
The accepted payment method is Pix or PayPal. 
Canceling will result in partial refund: 
(Draft 80%, Lineart 60%, Coloring 40%, Rendering 20%, Done 0%) 
After a stage of the art is completed, if any change is required, the price of that change is budget, accordingly, discussed between artist and client.
 
**Time to deliver**
Delivery minimal period is up to 1 month after Payment. 
If is there a lot of complexity, delivering time will be discussed. 

**Copyright** 
If you want me to copy anything from an artist you like, first step is to prove your permission to it, otherwise I'm limited to use as a source of inspiration only. For coping bigger companies characterize, its permitted unless it harms the fair-use policy. 

**Content**
I'm willing to draw anything. 
If a request makes me uncomfortable, I reserve the right to refuse it. 

**Style**
I'll not draw in other artist styles. I can try varying a little, but I refined my own way of art. 
I do not trace drawings by other artists. 

**Signature** 
All drawings will be delivered with my signature, as proof that the artwork was made by me. 
The signature must not be removed.

**Usage** 
Intended for personal use. 
If the artwork will be used to commercial use (e.g., prints, merch, etc.), 
there is going to need a contract. 
The buyer and no one else is allowed to make changes to my drawing.

**AI**
The artwork must not be used for AI training unless it have my personal permission.

**Confidentiality** 
Artwork may be posted unless client state clearly the need of confidentiality.
